Project Showcase

Selected projects demonstrating skills in Food Technology, Design & Technology, Industrial Technology, Textiles Technology, and Digital Design.

TECH213

In TECH213, our assignment was to create food moulds for a need. My project was to make a meal kit for chefs working in a nursing home. The main target market was residents with dysphagia who experience difficulty swallowing.

TECH306

For TECH306, we chose an everyday product, analysed it, and redesigned it. I worked on Woolworths Beef Lasagne, running tests to improve sustainability, nutrition, and appeal to a broader market.

TECH205

Veneer project: a small serving tray designed for two teacups and a miniature teapot, using a checker pattern of contrasting veneers.

TECH205

A footstool designed for Taronga Zoo’s merchandise store, inspired by the Arabian oryx to raise awareness of endangered species.

TECH108

Embroidery task incorporating multiple techniques on felt, with LED lights added to enhance appeal.

TECH104
